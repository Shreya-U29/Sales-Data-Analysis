# 📊 Sales Data Analysis – Python Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** on sales data to identify patterns in product performance, revenue generation, and sales distribution. The objective is to extract meaningful business insights that can help organizations understand **which products perform best, how sales vary over time, and where revenue opportunities exist**.

Using **Python and data analysis libraries**, the dataset was cleaned, analyzed, and visualized to uncover trends in sales performance.

---

## 📊 Data Source

The dataset contains transactional sales information including details about:

* Orders
* Products
* Revenue
* Time of purchase
* Customer purchasing behavior

### Key Columns in the Dataset

* `Order ID` – Unique identifier for each order
* `Product` – Product purchased
* `Quantity Ordered` – Number of units sold
* `Price Each` – Price per unit
* `Order Date` – Date of purchase
* `Purchase Address` – Customer purchase location

These attributes help analyze **sales trends, revenue generation, and customer purchasing patterns**.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Libraries Used

* **Python**
* **Pandas** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **NumPy** – Numerical computations
* **Matplotlib** – Data visualization
* **Seaborn** – Statistical visualizations

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Removed missing or invalid records
* Converted data types (dates, numeric fields)
* Created additional features for analysis such as:

  * Month
  * Hour of purchase
  * Sales revenue

---

### 2️⃣ Monthly Sales Analysis

* Calculated **total sales revenue per month**
* Identified months with **highest and lowest sales**

This helps understand **seasonal trends in business performance**.

---

### 3️⃣ Product Performance Analysis

* Identified **best-selling products**
* Compared total quantity sold for each product

This reveals which products drive the majority of revenue.

---

### 4️⃣ Sales by Time of Day

* Analyzed **orders by hour**
* Identified peak purchase times

This insight can help businesses determine **optimal advertising or promotional hours**.

---

### 5️⃣ City-wise Sales Analysis

* Extracted city information from purchase addresses
* Compared total sales across different cities

This helps identify **high-performing markets**.

---

### 6️⃣ Product Pair Analysis

* Examined products frequently purchased together
* Helps identify **bundling opportunities and cross-selling strategies**

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Certain months generate significantly higher revenue, indicating **seasonal demand patterns**.
* A small number of products contribute heavily to overall sales.
* Orders peak during specific hours of the day, suggesting ideal marketing times.
* Some cities consistently generate higher revenue.
* Certain products are frequently purchased together, indicating **bundling opportunities**.
